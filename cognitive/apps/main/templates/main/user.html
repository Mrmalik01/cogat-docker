4. DEFINE RELATIONSHIPS

The most important part of the ontology is defining relationships between concepts. If you think of the ontology like a graph, these are the branches that connect the nodes. They are essential. You can add a term to any kind of relationship by clicking on The ontology has the relationships "is a" and "part" of defined, for both parent and child terms. You can use the "Atlas view" to get intuition about these relationships, or look at an example.
